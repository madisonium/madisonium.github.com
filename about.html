Madisonium is a collection of active individuals that collaborate on ideas to improve the tech and web industries in Madison. We are pursuing non-profit status to help us more effectively fund projects.

The organization is lead by a board elected to 1 year terms.

The current board is:

  • Brad Grzesiak, Chair — Co-Founder, Bendyworks
  • Steve Faulkner, Vice Chair — Dev/UX, Murfie
  • Preston Austin — Co-Founder, Murfie
  • Zach Brandon — President, Greater Madison Chamber of Commerce
  • Grant Dobbe — Lead Developer and Managing Director, Binary Sprocket LLC
  • Timothy Nott — Co-Founder, MobileIgniter
  • Scott Resnick — Vice President, Hardin Design & Development
  • Forrest Woolworth — COO, PerBlue
  • Alnisa Allgood — Exec Dir, NonProfit Tech

Our former board members include:

  • Dave Cieslewicz — Former Mayor of Madison, Consultant, UW Lecturer
  • Lucas Dailey — CEO, Our Ballot Box
  • Bobby Voliva — CTO, Our Ballot Box
  • Andrew Shell — CTO, Pinpoint Software

More information on Elections, including how to submit yourself for nominations, can be found on our Elections page.
